BIL STUDENT ADVENTURE

A fun board game for students to disconnect from gadgets, play with friends, build tactics, and boost their knowledge!

About The Project

🎲 What it is

A Monopoly-style board game specifically designed for 2-6 players, tailored for school students.

⚠️ The Problem

High student dependence on digital screens and a lack of engaging face-to-face communication in dorms.

💡 Why it matters

It brings people together, improves communication, develops logical thinking, and helps learn new things through special tasks.

⭐ Uniqueness

A relatable school theme, custom design, and a perfect balance between having fun and educational elements.

How to Play & Game Logic

Goal: The primary goal is to be the first player to complete the path from START to FINISH.

Game Figures (Tokens):

🍞 Toast
🏆 Trophy
🐦 Bird
📖 Book
⚽ Ball
🧠 Brain

Special Cards

🟥 Косяк (Oops!)

The player must perform a penalty action described on the card.

🟩 Урок (Lesson)

An educational task or question to answer.

🟨 Бонус (Bonus)

A helpful reward or advantage for the player.

🟪 Усложнение

A temporary difficulty or obstacle for the player.

Sponsor Interview

Sponsor: Kuatbek Ilyas | Interviewers: Alinur Kopzhasar & Asyltay Alimansur

"I work in the field of education and I am interested in innovative and creative ideas. I enjoy supporting student projects because they help young people develop creativity, responsibility, and critical thinking."

"This project helps solve the problem of low student engagement and lack of creative educational activities. Many students spend too much time on digital entertainment."

"This project would be worth supporting if it encourages teamwork, logical thinking, and creativity. If students enjoy playing the game and learn something new from it, I would consider it a successful project."

"I believe the main target audience should be school students, especially teenagers. This age group is very active and curious."

"I expect the game to have clear rules, attractive design, and interesting gameplay. It should be easy to understand, fair, and engaging."

"My main concern is time management and complexity. Since this is a student project, it is important to keep the idea realistic and achievable."

"I would support this project if it is well-planned, creative, and original. My main advice is to stay focused, believe in your idea, and work consistently toward your goal."
